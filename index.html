Marble is a rock resulting from metamorphism of sedimentary carbonate rocks, most commonly limestone or dolomite
Metamorphism causes variable re-crystallization of the original carbonate
mineral grains. The resulting marble rock is typically composed of an interlocking mosaic of carbonate crystals
